We are business minded technology thought leaders

March 25, 2016by anas

Overview

Trustmarq Global Services resources are seasoned information assurance and risk management professionals with a thorough understanding of business needs, and a deep knowledge of the technology domain. They can effectively communicate with all levels of the organization. From conducting executive presentations to senior leadership, to moderating deep technical discussions; our consultants act as a bridge between business and IT, moderating and mapping the needs of the business with the IT capabilities.

Our consultants have a deep understanding of their respective domains of expertise, as well as a broad view of the business and IT landscape. This allows them to think out of the box and craft solutions that are driven by client business requirements.

Trustmarq Global Services consultants share the following professional attributes:

  • Professional integrity in every action
  • Business value-driven and results-oriented
  • Leaders, team players, with innovative problem-solving skills
  • Recognized by their clients and peers
  • Big4 consulting background, as well as in-depth operational experience
  • Highly qualified, knowledgeable and certified in their respective practicing domains
  • Cross industry knowledge and work experience
